一阶均差：f[x0,x1]=f(x1)−f(x0)x1−x0二阶均差：f[x0,x1,x2]=f[x1,x2]−f[x0,x1]x2−x0\begin{array}{l} 一阶均差：f\left[x_{0}, x_{1}\right]=\frac{f\left(x_{1}\right)-f\left(x_{0}\right)}{x_{1}-x_{0}} \\ 二阶均差：f\left[x_{0}, x_{1}, x_{2}\right]=\frac{f\left[x_{1}, x_{2}\right]-f\left[x_{0}, x_{1}\right]}{x_{2}-x_{0}} \end{array}一阶均差：f[x0​,x1​]=x1​−x0​f(x1​)−f(x0​)​二阶均差：f[x0​,x1​,x2​]=x2​−x0​f[x1​,x2​]−f[x0​,x1​]​​
